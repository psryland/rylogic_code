Clicket


Clicket is a simple little application for automatically "clicking" a button on a window. Say you're running a time consuming task on your PC overnight. When you return in the morning you discover that your task has barely started due to a message box asking "Are you sure?". Clicket simply watches for such dialogs and 'clicks' the appropriate button for you.
